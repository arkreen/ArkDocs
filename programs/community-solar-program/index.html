Community Solar Program

Objective​

To harness the power of the Web3 community and bring the vision of building DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks) together, Arkreen Network has introduced the Community Solar Program. This initiative encourages individuals worldwide who are concerned about climate change to integrate their own solar photovoltaic (PV) power systems into the Arkreen network. Doing so, we can build global renewable energy resources in a DePIN way and develop ReFi applications for public goods.

Application Process​

Interested applicants are invited to submit their applications by completing the Arkreen Community Solar Program Application Form.

The application process requires the following information for review:

  • Contact information, such as Email address and Telegram account.
  • Region of the power plant: country/region and city.
  • Photos of solar photovoltaic, inverter’s interface and nameplate etc.
  • Additional information, such as grid connection status, third-party monitoring service etc.

Important:

Individuals sharing personal solar photovoltaic photos must ensure they are authentic and comply with local laws. Images must accurately depict personal assets and adhere to regulations to maintain integrity in the Web3 community.

Benefits of Pioneering Pilot for Community Solar Program​

Arkreen Network is offering exclusive benefits to the pioneers of the Community Solar Program, including:

  1. Free Hardware Delivery and Three-Month Trial License: Upon approval, Arkreen will ship hardware devices, including data loggers or current transformer meters, designed for gathering renewable energy data from your solar panel system. Trial users will receive the hardware along with a three-month trial license. After the trial period, you can purchase the hardware if you wish to continue participating in the CSP program as a plant miner. Arkreen covers all delivery expenses; however, in some regions, applicants may need to manage customs clearance procedures.
  2. Technical Support: A dedicated technical team will provide 24/7 monitoring and troubleshooting support, ensuring the smooth operation and efficiency of participating community power plants.
  3. Arkreen DePIN+ ReFi EcoBoost: Pioneer participants will receive VIP access to exhibitions, demonstrations, news reports, and AMA sessions facilitated through Arkreen official accounts and partnerships in the Arkreen DePIN+ ReFi Ecosystem.
  4. Token Incentives for Plant Miners: We’re introducing token incentives for CSP plant miners, designed to encourage participation and recognize contributions from individuals who integrate their solar photovoltaic (PV) power plants into the Arkreen network. Stay tuned for further details as we develop the reward plan and principles.

The launch of the Arkreen Community Solar Program marks a significant milestone in the journey towards a sustainable and decentralized energy future. With the collective efforts of communities worldwide, Arkreen aims to revolutionize the way we harness and utilize solar energy for the betterment of society and the planet.

Eligible Criteria for Community Solar Program​

  • The total installed capacity of the solar PV system ranges from 2 kW to 40 kW.

  • Residential solar PV system fully owned by the applicant.

  • The solar PV system doesn't receive any carbon/renewable energy credits or subsidies.

  • Provide information as required by the Arkreen Network, including:

    • The link of Google Map for Latitude and longitude of the community solar PV system's location, ensuring visibility on satellite maps.
    • Overall photos of the community solar PV system, including all photovoltaic panels.
    • Overall photos of the inverters installed in the community solar PV system, photos of the inverter interfaces, and photos of the inverter nameplates.
    • Optionally, additional authorization information for API mode access, such as usernames/passwords, API keys, Access Tokens, etc.

  • Accept Arkreen Network requirements for the public information disclosure about community solar PV system, including:

    • Location of the community solar PV system, specifying the country/region and city.
    • Overall photos of the community solar PV system.

Sales of Remote Miner under Community Solar PV System​

  • The data of community solar PV system is split into remote miners based on 80% of the installed capacity, using 100W as the unit. For example, a 10 kW community solar PV system can be split into 80 remote miners. The remaining 20% of installed capacity is reserved as a replacement target for remote miners belonging to other community solar PV systems. Please refer to Quality Assurance Mechanism for Community Solar PV system.
  • Remote miners split from community solar PV system are listed on Arkreen Console for sale, and sales revenue belongs to the Arkreen Foundation.
  • Remote miners under community solar PV system enjoy the same rights as remote miners issued by Arkreen.

Reward Rules for Community Solar PV System​

  • The reward quantity received by a community solar PV system is 50% of the hourly reward quantity for remote miners under that community solar PV system.
  • In the case of a community solar PV system change, the belonging solar PV system for remote miners is the one after the last solar PV system change. If no change occurs, the belonging solar PV system is the one selected by the community member during the purchase.

Access Modes for Community Solar PV System​

Arkreen selects the appropriate access mode based on the specific situation of the community solar PV system:

  • CT meter mode, requiring the installation of CT meter, suitable for all AC output community solar PV system.
    • In the initial stage, Arkreen will ship the CT meter hardware to selected community member for 3 months trial. After 3 months trial, community member has the option to purchase hardware to officially onboard this hardware to Arkreen Network. Detailed rules will be announced later.
  • Smart Plug mode, requiring the installation of smart plug, suitable for single-phase AC output community power stations.
    • In the initial stage, will ship the Smart Plug hardware to selected community member for 3 months trial. After 3 months trial, community member has the option to purchase hardware to officially onboard this hardware to Arkreen Network. Detailed rules will be announced later.
  • API mode, suitable for community solar PV system with Enphase Microinverters.
    • In the initial stage, Arkreen waives device onboarding fees and provides a 3-month mining validity period. Users can also submit the application for new inverter brand support and provide relevant authorizations, such as usernames/passwords, API keys, Access Tokens, etc. After the expiration of the usage period, users have the option to choose to pay extension fees to convert to an unlimited validity period. Detailed rules will be announced later.

Quality Assurance Mechanism for Community Solar PV System​

  • If a community solar PV system has no generation data reporting for three consecutive days due to network issues or other reasons, or if the generated power is significantly lower than its installed capacity (excluding seasonal and weather factors), the remote miner belonging to it will be replaced with another solar PV system, and this community solar PV system will be delisted from the shop on Arkreen Console.
  • In the future, community solar PV system will be required to stake a corresponding amount of AKRE based on their installed capacity and access mode. Specific rules will be announced later.
